# Sales & Order Tracking MIS Report

## Overview

This project showcases an Excel-based Management Information System (MIS) report developed to monitor sales performance, order tracking, regional performance, sales channels, and product profitability. The report transforms raw sales data into structured management reports, enabling business leaders to track KPIs, identify performance gaps, and make data-driven decisions.

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to automate sales reporting and provide management with timely insights into sales performance across regions, channels, and product categories. The report helps identify underperforming areas, monitor target achievement, and support strategic business planning.
